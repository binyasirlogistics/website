Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our dispatching services

How quickly can I start working with Binyasir Logistics?

We can have you up and running within 24-48 hours after completing your carrier setup. Our onboarding process is streamlined to get you earning as quickly as possible.

What documents do I need to get started?

You'll need your MC authority, insurance certificates, W9 form, and signed carrier agreement. We'll help you gather any missing documentation during setup.

Do you work with owner-operators and small fleets?

Absolutely! We specialize in working with owner-operators and small to medium-sized fleets. Our personalized approach ensures each driver gets the attention they deserve.

What are your payment terms?

Our fees are collected after you get paid for each load. We offer multiple pricing options including per-load, percentage, and weekly rates to fit your preference.

Can I choose my own loads and routes?

Yes! We present you with the best available options and you have final approval on every load. We work with your preferences for routes, load types, and schedule.
