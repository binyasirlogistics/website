About FastTrack Dispatch

Your trusted partner in truck dispatching services

Our Story

FastTrack Dispatch was founded in 2010 by a team of industry veterans who recognized the challenges faced by independent truck drivers and small fleet owners in navigating the complex freight market.

What began as a small operation serving local carriers has grown into a nationwide dispatching service trusted by hundreds of trucking professionals. Our growth has been fueled by our unwavering commitment to our clients' success and our deep understanding of the trucking industry.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our services while maintaining the personalized approach that has become our hallmark.
