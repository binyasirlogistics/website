Carrier Requirements

To ensure the highest standards of service and safety, we have the following requirements for our carrier partners:

  • Valid DOT and MC numbers
  • Current commercial auto insurance (minimum $1M)
  • Cargo insurance (minimum $100K)
  • Clean safety record and compliance history
  • Professional drivers with valid CDL
  • Reliable communication equipment
  • GPS tracking capability
  • ELD compliance

Don't meet all requirements yet? Contact us to discuss how we can help you get qualified.
