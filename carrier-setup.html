Required Documents

To complete the setup process, you will need to provide the following documents:

  • MC Authority
  • Certificate of Liability Insurance ($1,000,000 minimum)
  • Certificate of Cargo Insurance ($100,000 minimum)
  • Completed W-9 Form
  • Copy of CDL (for owner-operators)
  • Voided Check or Company Banking Information

You can email these documents to setup@fasttackdispatch.com or upload them through our secure setup form below.

Setup FAQs

Common questions about our setup process

How long does the setup process take?

Once we receive your completed application and all required documents, we can typically complete the setup process within 24-48 hours. You can start receiving load options as soon as your setup is completed.

Is there a fee to sign up?

Yes, there is a one-time setup fee of $150 to cover the administrative costs of setting up your account with our system and with our broker network. This fee is payable once your application is approved.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

No, our service agreements are month-to-month. We don't believe in locking carriers into long-term contracts. You can terminate the agreement with 15 days' written notice if you are not satisfied with our services.

What if I don't have all the required documents ready?

You can still submit your application, but we'll need all required documents before we can complete your setup and start finding loads. Our team can guide you through obtaining any missing documents if needed.

Can I update my preferences after setup?

Absolutely! We understand that your business needs and preferences may change over time. You can update your preferences at any time by contacting your assigned dispatcher or our customer support team.
