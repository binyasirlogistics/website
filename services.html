Common Questions

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

How quickly will I hear back after contacting you?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly.

What information should I have ready when I contact you?

Having information about your equipment type, preferred lanes, and current dispatching setup will help us provide the most relevant assistance during our initial conversation.

Can I schedule a consultation before signing up?

Absolutely! We offer free consultations to discuss your specific needs and how our services can benefit your business. You can schedule one through our contact form or by calling us directly.

How do I get started with your dispatching services?

The best way to get started is to fill out our contact form or visit our Carrier Setup page. We'll guide you through the process, which includes completing our carrier profile, providing necessary documentation, and signing our service agreement.
